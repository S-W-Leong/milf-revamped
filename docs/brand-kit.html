01 — Palette

Colour

Dark, quiet surfaces. Sage-pearl is the only chromatic colour, and it appears only when something has been confirmed or completed. Click any swatch to copy its value.

Tap a swatch to copy the hex / value.

The voice arc — grey to sage

Voice begins as ambient grey (raw human intent) and resolves into sage when the task completes. The signature motif: restraint, then resolution.

02 — Logo

The mark

A minimal waveform — one breath, two endpoints — on a dark, square-radius badge. Premium on any home screen. The sage dot is the only colour: a full stop that means "done."

03 — Typography

Type

Geometric sans in the Inter / Geist family — clean and precise, never a serif, never condensed. Large body text reads as confidence, not accommodation.
